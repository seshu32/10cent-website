# 10cent Website Development Guidelines
# ======================================

## Development Workflow

Follow these steps for each section:

1. **ANALYZE** - When user says "Now design this section", carefully analyze the provided design image.

2. **GATHER REQUIREMENTS** - Ask user for any required assets:
   - Images (product photos, illustrations, backgrounds)
   - Logos (brand logo, favicon)
   - Brand colors (hex codes)
   - Fonts (if specific fonts are required)
   - Icons (if specific icons are needed)

3. **DEVELOP** - Build the section in Node.js, replicating the design as closely as possible.

## Design Rules (Established)

### Header Section
- Logo on left side
- "JOIN THE CLUB" button on right side
  - Black background (#000000)
  - White text (#FFFFFF)
  - Font size: 13px
  - Font weight: 400
  - Text alignment: left
  - No hover effects
- Fixed header at top
- Responsive for mobile/tablet

### Hero Section
- Layout: 3-column grid (left image, center text, right images)
- Left image: Person by stream
- Center text: "30 nights a year." + tagline
- Right images: 2 stacked images (cottage, person in forest)
- Bottom: 10% logo + "CLUB" text + "THE ART OF RETURN"
- Images via URLs (external hosting)
- Font weight: 400
- Responsive layout (stacks on mobile)

### The Great Disconnect Section
- Layout: Split screen (50/50)
- Left side: Full-bleed nature/waterfall image with centered overlay text
  - Title: "THE GREAT DISCONNECT" (80px, white, uppercase)
  - Subtitle: "We are a species in exile." (18px, white)
- Right side: Content panel with beige background (#F5F5F0)
  - Top quote about success
  - "THE URBAN TRAP" section with statistics
  - Middle text about urban professionals
  - Bottom statement about anxiety
- Bottom: Two quote boxes side-by-side
  - "THE QUIET PANIC" and "THE REALITY"
  - Light beige background (#F9F9F7)
  - Italic quote text
- Responsive: Stacks vertically on tablet/mobile
- Full viewport height on desktop

### Under The Trees Section
- Placement: After "This is not for" section
- Background: Full-bleed forest image
- Text overlays:
  - "WE HAVE HELD A SEAT"
  - "UNDER THE TREES FOR YOU."
  - Supporting line at bottom-left (small text)
- Hover state:
  - Dark overlay over the full section
  - Semi-transparent focus rectangle in the center area
- Responsive behavior:
  - Preserve text hierarchy and relative positioning on tablet/mobile
